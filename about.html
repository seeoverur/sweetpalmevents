About Us

We Want the Chance to Earn Your Business

Sweet Palm Private Events is a catering company based in Charleston, SC. We're not the biggest name in the room — but we show up, we care about the details, and we genuinely want to be the caterer that makes your event run well.

What We're About

We are the catering branch of Sweet Palm Coffee & Food, a local coffee shop at 471 King Street in Charleston. We started doing catering because the same guests who loved our coffee kept asking us to bring that experience to their events — and it made sense. Food and drink that people actually enjoy, served by people who actually care.

We cater all types of events — office lunches, corporate functions, weddings, private parties, fundraisers, and public gatherings. Every event gets its own conversation. There's no standard package we push on anyone, because a team lunch for 20 people and a 150-person wedding reception are completely different jobs that deserve completely different planning.

If you're looking for a caterer and want to hear what we can do for your specific event, we'd genuinely like the opportunity to talk. Call us, tell us what you're working on, and we'll give you a straight answer on whether we're the right fit and what it would cost.

How We Work with Clients

Our process is straightforward. You call or message us, we learn about your event, and we put together a plan that fits. No online forms that go nowhere, no generic quotes — just a real conversation with people who want to help you pull it off.

📞
You reach out — we listen first

We don't start with a price sheet. We start by asking what your event actually needs. Every catering job is different and we treat it that way.

🍽
We build around your event, not a template

Menu, service style, timing, headcount — all of it gets designed around what works for your specific event and your specific guests.

💬
Pricing is honest and discussed by phone

We price every job based on what it actually requires. Call us and we'll give you a straightforward number — no hidden fees, no surprises on the day.

✅
We show up and handle the details

On the day of your event, we take care of the food and drink side so you can focus on your guests. That's the job and we take it seriously.

Give Us a Shot

We're not going to oversell ourselves. What we can tell you is that we're a local Charleston business that cares about the work, takes catering seriously, and wants to build a reputation one well-fed event at a time. If you have an event coming up — office lunch, wedding, party, fundraiser, whatever it is — call us and let us make the case.
