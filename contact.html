Common Questions

A few things people usually ask before reaching out.

How do I get a price for my event?

Call or text us at 843-327-0347 and tell us about your event — date, location, and approximate guest count. Every event is priced individually, so a quick conversation is the fastest way to get a real number.

What types of events do you cater?

We cater office lunches, corporate functions, weddings, private parties, birthday celebrations, fundraisers, and public gatherings. If you have an event with food and drink needs, reach out and we'll let you know if we can help.

Do you have set menus or packages?

We don't use fixed packages. Every menu is built around the specific event — headcount, event type, and what you'd like to serve. We'll discuss options when you call.

How far in advance should I book?

As early as possible for larger events. For smaller office or private gatherings we can sometimes accommodate shorter timelines. Call us to check availability for your date.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Charleston and the surrounding Lowcountry including Mount Pleasant, West Ashley, James Island, Daniel Island, Hanahan, Goose Creek, and Summerville.

Do you handle both food and drinks?

Yes. We offer food catering, beverage service, and coffee service. The combination depends on your event — we'll sort that out in our first conversation.
